## Design Notes

- **Format:** 3 slides, 1080×1080, dark background (#0D0D0D)
- **Hook style:** "S-tier [topic]" or "F-tier [topic]" — bold, provocative, ranking-based
- **Info slide:** 2 bullets max, → arrow prefix, scannable
- **CTA slide:** App name + one-line value prop + bold comment trigger
- **Consistent:** @jaidenrabatin bottom-right, amber accent (#F5A623), Inter font
- **Tone:** Direct, slightly provocative, no fluff. Like a smart friend who doesn't waste your time.
- **S-tier = good/aspirational.** F-tier = bad/warning. The contrast creates curiosity.
