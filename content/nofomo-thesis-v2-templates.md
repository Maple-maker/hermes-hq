## Hook Framework Reference

| Framework | Formula | Best For |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| Contradiction | "[Negative], but [positive]" | Creating tension |
| Specificity | Hyper-specific, relatable details | Credibility |
| Timeframe | "[Long result] in [short time]" | Curiosity loop |
| POV | "POV: you [relatable scenario]" | Advice in disguise |
| Authority | "Most people X. We Y." | Contrast + credibility |
| "This is what happens" | "This is what happens when X" | Consequence + curiosity |

**Pro tip:** Combine 2-3 frameworks per hook for maximum stop-power.
