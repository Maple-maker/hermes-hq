# Claude Design Prompt — /brand-new-day Instagram Carousel

**Output:** 7 slide images, 1080×1080px, PNG or high-quality JPG
**Style:** Dark, sleek, Spider-Man "Brand New Day" movie poster aesthetic. Red and black with amber accents. Think the Spider-Man: Brand New Day title card — bold, cinematic, slightly gritty. Web motifs, morning light breaking through darkness.
**Consistency rules across all slides:**
- Same background color on every slide: #0D0D0D (near-black)
- Same font family throughout: Inter or Space Grotesk (bold for headlines, regular for body)
- Red accent (Spider-Man red): #E23636 — used for key highlights, the "web" thread, and emphasis
- Amber accent: #F5A623 — used for the "morning sun" / new day metaphor
- Each slide has a thin red or amber rule line separating the headline from the body
- Bottom-right corner of every slide: small "@jaidenrabatin" in 60% opacity white
- Subtle web pattern in corners of each slide (thin white lines at low opacity)

## Additional Design Specifications

**Color Reference Card:**
| Element | Hex |
|---------|-----|
| Background | #0D0D0D |
| White text | #FAFAFA |
| Spider-Man red (accents, web, emphasis) | #E23636 |
| Amber accent (sunrise, new day) | #F5A623 |
| Safe path green | #22C55E |
| Subtle text / rules | #FFFFFF at 40-60% opacity |

**Typography:**
- Headline: 48-56pt, bold (700), white #FAFAFA, tracking -0.5
- Subhead: 28-32pt, semibold (600), amber #F5A623
- Body: 18-22pt, regular (400), white #FAFAFA or 80% opacity, line height 1.4
- Code/monospace: 16-18pt, JetBrains Mono or Fira Code, red #E23636 on #1A1A1A background
- Small labels: 14pt, 50-60% opacity white, uppercase tracking +1

**Grid / Layout:**
- 48px safe margins on all sides
- Headline area: top 25% of slide
- Body area: middle 50%
- Footer/tag area: bottom 15%
- Thin horizontal red or amber rule (1px, 40% opacity) between headline and body on every slide

**Visual Motifs (recurring across all slides):**
1. Web strands — thin white lines in corners at low opacity (Spider-Man motif)
2. Sunrise/morning light — amber glow representing "new day"
3. The red/amber rule line separating headline from body
4. Small @jaidenrabatin in bottom-right corner, every slide
