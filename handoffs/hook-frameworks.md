# 🎣 Hook Frameworks — From Reel Transcript Analysis

> **Source:** Instagram reel transcript + Reddit thread on S-tier templates
> **Core insight:** Hooks with contradictions get the work done. Your brain can't scroll past unresolved tension.

---

## Framework 1: Contradiction Hook

**Formula:** "[Negative state], but [positive action/desire]"

**Why it works:** Creates unresolved tension. The brain can't scroll past it — it needs to resolve the contradiction.

**Examples from the wild:**
- "I'm drunk, but Imma do my best to tell this story"
- "Terrified? Absolutely. Ready? Not really. Worth it? 100%."
- "This is what happens when you let AI set up your server and nobody knows what they're doing. Twenty-two thousand — that's the bill."

**NoFomo applications:**
- "The market says sell. The data says buy. Here's who's right."
- "CNBC says it's over. The insider cluster says it's just starting."
- "The stock dropped 15%. The thesis didn't change. Here's what that means."

**Thesis applications:**
- "Everyone says diversify. I went all-in on one thesis. Here's what happened."
- "The bear case is terrifying. The position size is tiny. That's the point."

---

## Framework 2: Specificity Effect

**Formula:** Replace generic statements with hyper-specific, relatable details

**Why it works:** Hyper-specificity creates instant credibility. People's brains go, "This person actually lived this."

**Generic → Specific:**
- "If you ever get bloated after a meal..." → "If you've ever secretly unbuttoned your jeans at dinner and hoped no one noticed — this is for you"
- "If you ever lose money trading..." → "If you've ever watched a stock drop 20% on no news and panic sold right before it doubled..."

**NoFomo applications:**
- Generic: "If you want to find alpha before everyone else..."
- Specific: "If you've ever seen a stock pop 40% and thought 'I should have seen that coming' — here's the signal cluster that flagged it 3 weeks earlier."

**Thesis applications:**
- Generic: "If you want to write better investment theses..."
- Specific: "If you've ever written a thesis that started with 'I like this company' and got laughed out of the room — here's the framework that actually gets funded."

---

## Framework 3: Timeframe Tension

**Formula:** "[Long time] of [result] in [short time]"

**Why it works:** Unexpected timeframes create a dopamine hit. Short, punchy timeframes have major viral potential. Kicks off an elite curiosity loop and gives the viewer hope.

**Examples:**
- "3 years of back progress in 30 seconds"
- "Three months ago I had 0 followers, today I'm at 211K"
- "Twenty-two thousand — that's the bill" (implied: in a very short time)

**NoFomo applications:**
- "63 trading days. That's how long the average catalyst takes to fully reprice. Most people sell at day 5."
- "14 days. That's how long it takes for 2+ insiders to cluster-buy before the market notices."
- "3 weeks. That's how long a $50M government contract takes to get priced in. The stock moved 2% on day one. It moved 12% by week 3."

**Thesis applications:**
- "60 seconds. That's how long it takes to know if your thesis is falsifiable or just a vibe."
- "One sentence. That's all it takes to know if your thesis is worth funding."

---

## Framework 4: POV = Advice in Disguise

**Formula:** "POV: you [relatable scenario that secretly teaches something]"

**Why it works:** People's defenses are down when they think they're just relating to a scenario, not receiving instruction.

**Examples:**
- "POV: you figured out how to not pay a fortune for drinks at festivals"
- "POV: You don't feel like cooking, but still want a home-cooked meal"

**NoFomo applications:**
- "POV: You see a stock drop 15% on 'bad news' but the insider cluster just bought $2M worth"
- "POV: CNBC is screaming sell, but your radar score just hit 83"
- "POV: You found a catalyst the market hasn't noticed yet and you have 3 weeks before it reprices"

**Thesis applications:**
- "POV: You wrote a thesis with actual invalidation conditions and the investor actually read it"
- "POV: Your position size is based on math, not gut feel"

---

## Framework 5: The "This Is What Happens When..." Hook

**Formula:** "This is what happens when you [do X] and [Y goes wrong/right]"

**Why it works:** Immediate consequence + curiosity. The viewer needs to know what happened.

**From the transcript:**
- "This is what happens when you let AI set up your server and nobody knows what they're doing. Twenty-two thousand — that's the bill."
- "This is what you get when you try to do it yourself, or you hire twenty-year-olds who've never developed a real production system a day in their lives."

**NoFomo applications:**
- "This is what happens when you trade on CNBC headlines instead of primary sources."
- "This is what happens when you ignore the reprice gap and sell too early."
- "This is what happens when you let the AI council do the research and you just approve the thesis."

**Thesis applications:**
- "This is what happens when you write a falsifiable thesis for the first time."
- "This is what happens when you size your position based on bear case severity instead of conviction."

---

## Framework 6: The "Kids So Green" Authority Hook

**Formula:** Contrast between the people who don't know and the people who do. Establishes authority through contrast.

**From the transcript:**
- "Kids so green their balls haven't even dropped yet. They can prompt AI all day, but they don't know DevOps."
- "AI didn't just cost them twenty-two thousand. Letting clueless people play DevOps in production did."

**Why it works:** Creates an "us vs. them" dynamic. The viewer wants to be on the side that knows.

**NoFomo applications:**
- "Most people read the headline. We read the filing. Here's the difference."
- "They see a stock drop. We see an overreaction reversal setup. Here's the data."

---

## The Master Formula (Combining Frameworks)

The best hooks combine 2-3 frameworks:

**Example 1: Contradiction + Specificity + Timeframe**
> "The stock dropped 15% in 5 days. The thesis didn't change. The insiders just bought $2M. Here's what happens next."

**Example 2: POV + Contradiction + Timeframe**
> "POV: You found a catalyst the market hasn't noticed. You have 3 weeks before it reprices. Most people sell at day 5."

**Example 3: "This is what happens" + Authority + Specificity**
> "This is what happens when you trade on vibes instead of verifiable evidence. Your hit rate is 34%. Ours is 68%. Same market. Different process."

---

## How to Use This

1. **Pick a framework** that matches your content angle
2. **Fill in the specifics** from your data/features
3. **Keep it to 1-2 sentences** for the hook slide
4. **Follow with 2 info bullets** that deliver on the promise
5. **Close with CTA** that ties back to the hook
